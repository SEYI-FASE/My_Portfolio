How it began!

In a surprising turn of events, I, a Firefighter with a background as an Underwriter has made a remarkable career transition into the world of data analysis. Recognizing the demand for data-driven decision making in multiple industries, I have embraced the power of continuous learning and used online platforms such as DataCamp, Coursera, and Udemy to equip myself with the necessary skills to pave way into the data analytics field.

While the journey from fighting fires, which has equipped me with the Problem-solving mindset, to analyzing data may seem like a significant leap, my unwavering determination, combined with newly acquired data analysis skills to make informed decisions based on data insights acquired from the convenience of online learning platforms, facilitated a successful transition.
